T.Rex -
The Slider

Release Notes

  • Released: 21 July 1972
  • Recorded: March–April 1972
  • Studio: Rosenberg Studios, Copenhagen, Denmark; Château d'Hérouville, Paris, France; Elektra Sound Recorders Studios, 962 La Cienega Boulevard, Los Angeles, California, 90069, United States

The Slider is the seventh studio album by English rock band T. Rex, released on 21 July 1972 by record labels EMI and Reprise. Two singles, "Telegram Sam" and "Metal Guru", were released to promote the album. The Slider received acclaim from critics, and reached number 4 in the UK charts and number 17 in the US.
